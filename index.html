Fun Cat Facts

  • Cats can rotate their ears 180 degrees!
  • They sleep for about 16 hours a day on average.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Cats have five toes on their front paws, but only four on their back paws.
  • The world's oldest cat lived to be 38 years old!

The Benefits of Owning a Cat

Owning a cat can bring joy and companionship into your life. Studies have shown that having a cat can reduce stress and anxiety levels.

How to Care for Your Cat

Caring for a cat involves more than just feeding them. Regular vet visits, grooming, and playtime are essential for a happy and healthy cat.
